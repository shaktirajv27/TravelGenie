Frequently Asked Questions

How does TravelGenie's AI recommendation work?

TravelGenie uses advanced machine learning algorithms to analyze your preferences, budget, travel dates, and interests to provide personalized recommendations. Our AI considers factors like weather, local events, travel trends, and user reviews to suggest the perfect destinations and activities for you.

Is TravelGenie free to use?

TravelGenie offers both free and premium plans. Our basic features including AI recommendations and itinerary planning are free. Premium features like advanced customization, priority support, and exclusive deals require a subscription.

Can I book directly through TravelGenie?

Currently, TravelGenie provides recommendations and connects you with trusted booking partners. We're working on integrated booking functionality to make your experience even more seamless.

How accurate are the budget estimates?

Our budget estimates are based on real-time data from multiple sources and historical travel costs. While we strive for accuracy, actual costs may vary based on season, availability, and personal preferences. We recommend using our estimates as a starting point for your planning.
