Getting Started

What is TravelGenie and how does it work?

TravelGenie is an AI-powered travel planning platform that creates personalized travel recommendations based on your preferences, budget, and travel style. Our advanced algorithms analyze thousands of destinations, activities, and accommodations to suggest the perfect trip for you.

Simply tell us your travel preferences, and we'll generate a customized itinerary with recommendations for flights, hotels, restaurants, and activities.

Do I need to create an account to use TravelGenie?

While you can browse our platform without an account, creating a free account unlocks many benefits:

  • Save and organize your travel plans
  • Access personalized recommendations
  • Share itineraries with friends and family
  • Receive price alerts and travel updates
  • Track your travel history and preferences
Is TravelGenie free to use?

TravelGenie offers both free and premium plans:

  • Free Plan: Basic AI recommendations, itinerary planning, and budget estimates
  • Premium Plan: Advanced customization, priority support, exclusive deals, and unlimited plan saves

You can start with our free plan and upgrade anytime if you need additional features.

Account & Profile

How do I update my profile information?

To update your profile information:

  • Log in to your TravelGenie account
  • Click on your profile picture or name in the top right corner
  • Select "Profile Settings" from the dropdown menu
  • Update your information and click "Save Changes"

Keep your profile updated to receive more personalized travel recommendations.

How do I change my password?

To change your password:

  • Go to Profile Settings
  • Click on "Security" tab
  • Enter your current password
  • Enter and confirm your new password
  • Click "Update Password"

For security, we recommend using a strong password with at least 8 characters, including uppercase and lowercase letters, numbers, and symbols.

Can I delete my account?

Yes, you can delete your TravelGenie account at any time:

  • Go to Account Settings > Privacy & Security
  • Scroll down to "Delete Account" section
  • Click "Delete My Account" and confirm

Warning: This action is permanent and cannot be undone. All your saved travel plans, preferences, and data will be permanently removed.

Travel Planning

How accurate are TravelGenie's recommendations?

Our AI recommendations are based on:

  • Real-time data from multiple travel sources
  • User reviews and ratings from verified travelers
  • Historical travel patterns and trends
  • Your personal preferences and travel history

While we strive for 95%+ accuracy, we always recommend verifying important details like visa requirements, weather conditions, and booking availability before making final decisions.

Can I customize the AI-generated itinerary?

Absolutely! You have full control over your itinerary:

  • Add, remove, or rearrange activities
  • Adjust timing and duration of visits
  • Change accommodation preferences
  • Modify budget allocations
  • Add personal notes and reminders

The AI learns from your modifications to provide better recommendations for future trips.

How far in advance should I plan my trip?

The ideal planning timeline depends on your destination:

  • Domestic trips: 2-8 weeks in advance
  • International trips: 2-6 months in advance
  • Peak season destinations: 6-12 months in advance
  • Last-minute trips: 1-2 weeks (limited options but possible deals)

Early planning gives you access to better prices and availability, especially for popular destinations and peak travel times.

Booking & Reservations

Can I book directly through TravelGenie?

Currently, TravelGenie partners with trusted booking platforms to provide you with the best deals:

  • We redirect you to verified booking partners
  • All bookings are made directly with hotels, airlines, and activity providers
  • We track prices and notify you of deals and price drops
  • Integrated booking functionality is coming soon!
Do you offer price matching or guarantees?

While TravelGenie doesn't directly handle bookings, we help you find the best prices by:

  • Comparing prices across multiple platforms
  • Setting up price alerts for your desired trips
  • Showing historical price trends
  • Recommending optimal booking times

For price matching, you'll need to work directly with the booking platform or provider.

What if I need to cancel or change my bookings?

Since bookings are made through our partner platforms:

  • Cancellation and change policies vary by provider
  • Contact the booking platform or provider directly
  • We recommend booking flexible rates when possible
  • Consider travel insurance for added protection

Always read the cancellation and change policies before booking to understand your options.

Technical Support

Which browsers are supported by TravelGenie?

TravelGenie works best on modern browsers:

  • Recommended: Chrome (latest), Firefox (latest), Safari (latest), Edge (latest)
  • Mobile: Chrome Mobile, Safari Mobile, Samsung Internet
  • Minimum requirements: Chrome 90+, Firefox 88+, Safari 14+, Edge 90+

For the best experience, please keep your browser updated to the latest version.

Why is the website loading slowly?

Slow loading can be caused by several factors:

  • Slow internet connection
  • Browser cache issues (try clearing cache and cookies)
  • Too many browser tabs or applications running
  • Outdated browser version
  • High traffic on our servers (rare)

Try refreshing the page (Ctrl+F5) or switching to an incognito/private browsing window.

Is there a TravelGenie mobile app?

We're currently developing native mobile apps for iOS and Android:

  • Beta testing will begin in Q2 2025
  • Full launch planned for Q3 2025
  • Meanwhile, our website is fully mobile-optimized
  • You can add TravelGenie to your home screen as a web app

Sign up for our newsletter to be notified when the app launches!
